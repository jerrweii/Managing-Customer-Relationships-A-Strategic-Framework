# Skill 20: Relationship Governance

## Purpose

Define who has authority to make customer-level decisions across teams, channels, and systems.

## When To Use

- Designing enterprise CRM workflows.
- Resolving sales, support, marketing, and success ownership conflicts.
- Building approvals or customer decision rights.

## Inputs

- Team responsibilities.
- Customer ownership rules.
- Decision types.
- Approval policies.
- Escalation paths.

## Repeatable Steps

1. Identify decisions that affect customer relationships.
2. Assign owners for each decision type.
3. Define approval and escalation rules.
4. Make decision rights visible in the CRM.
5. Audit whether decisions follow governance.

## Software Artifacts

- Decision rights matrix.
- Ownership model.
- Approval workflow.
- Escalation policy.
- Governance dashboard.

## Acceptance Checks

- Customer-level decisions have clear owners.
- Conflicting team incentives are visible.
- Important decisions are auditable.
- Governance supports customer value, not only internal control.
