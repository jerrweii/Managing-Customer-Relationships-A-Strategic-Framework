# Skill 12: Customer Insight Generation

## Purpose

Convert CRM data into actionable insight for employees, automation, and management.

## When To Use

- Building dashboards.
- Designing customer summaries.
- Creating opportunity, churn, health, or satisfaction indicators.

## Inputs

- Customer profile.
- Interaction history.
- Value signals.
- Usage data.
- Feedback and sentiment.

## Repeatable Steps

1. Define the decision the insight should support.
2. Select data signals tied to that decision.
3. Produce insight at the right grain: customer, account, segment, or portfolio.
4. Show evidence and confidence when insight drives action.
5. Track whether acted-on insight improves outcomes.

## Software Artifacts

- Customer summary.
- Health score.
- Churn and opportunity indicators.
- Insight dashboard.
- Evidence trail for recommendations.

## Acceptance Checks

- Insight is tied to a decision or action.
- Employees can understand why the system surfaced it.
- Stale or low-confidence insight is marked clearly.
- Insight quality is measured after action.
