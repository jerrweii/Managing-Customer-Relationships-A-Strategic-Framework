# Skill 13: Social Listening and Feedback Loops

## Purpose

Treat customer feedback, complaints, reviews, and social media signals as structured CRM input.

## When To Use

- Building voice-of-customer systems.
- Integrating social, survey, review, or support feedback.
- Detecting customer sentiment and issues.

## Inputs

- Feedback channels.
- Social listening sources.
- Survey data.
- Support complaints.
- Product reviews.

## Repeatable Steps

1. Ingest feedback with source and timestamp.
2. Link feedback to known customers when appropriate and permitted.
3. Classify topic, sentiment, urgency, and product or service area.
4. Route feedback to responsible teams.
5. Close the loop by recording resolution or response.

## Software Artifacts

- Feedback ingestion pipeline.
- Topic and sentiment taxonomy.
- Voice-of-customer dashboard.
- Case creation rules.
- Feedback resolution workflow.

## Acceptance Checks

- Feedback becomes actionable CRM data.
- Sensitive or public-source data respects consent and policy.
- The system distinguishes individual issues from trend signals.
- Teams can see whether feedback was resolved.
