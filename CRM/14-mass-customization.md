# Skill 14: Mass Customization

## Purpose

Use customer knowledge to vary treatment, offers, service, content, and workflow at scale.

## When To Use

- Designing personalization.
- Building next-best-action systems.
- Creating configurable customer journeys.

## Inputs

- Customer identity.
- Value tier.
- Needs and preferences.
- Interaction history.
- Product or service catalog.

## Repeatable Steps

1. Define which parts of treatment can vary.
2. Connect variation to customer value, need, consent, and context.
3. Implement rules or models that select treatment.
4. Log the reason for each customized action.
5. Measure whether customization improves customer and business outcomes.

## Software Artifacts

- Personalization service.
- Treatment catalog.
- Next-best-action rules.
- Offer eligibility engine.
- Customization audit log.

## Acceptance Checks

- Customization is based on relevant customer knowledge.
- Treatment choices are explainable and testable.
- Customers are not over-personalized in trust-breaking ways.
- Outcomes are measured against non-customized baselines.
