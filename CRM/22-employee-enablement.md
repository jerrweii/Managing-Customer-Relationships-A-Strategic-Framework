# Skill 22: Employee Enablement

## Purpose

Design CRM so employees can make better customer decisions with less friction.

## When To Use

- Building agent, seller, marketer, or success workspaces.
- Designing workflow automation.
- Improving CRM adoption.

## Inputs

- Employee roles.
- Customer decisions employees make.
- Current workflow pain points.
- Required customer context.
- Action and escalation paths.

## Repeatable Steps

1. Identify the customer decision each role must make.
2. Show only the context needed for that decision.
3. Provide guided actions, next steps, and relevant history.
4. Reduce duplicate entry and context switching.
5. Capture employee feedback to improve workflows.

## Software Artifacts

- Role-based workspace.
- Customer context panel.
- Guided workflow.
- Playbooks.
- Task and handoff model.

## Acceptance Checks

- Employees can see why an action is recommended.
- Customer context is available at the point of work.
- Workflow reduces manual effort or repeated lookup.
- Employee actions update customer memory.
