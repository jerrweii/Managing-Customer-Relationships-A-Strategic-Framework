# Skill 07: Needs Differentiation

## Purpose

Differentiate customers by what they are trying to accomplish, not just by demographics or purchase history.

## When To Use

- Designing personalization.
- Building customer success workflows.
- Creating segmentation beyond firmographics.

## Inputs

- Jobs-to-be-done research.
- Support topics.
- Product usage patterns.
- Customer goals.
- Preference data.

## Repeatable Steps

1. Identify recurring customer jobs, goals, and constraints.
2. Add needs and preference fields to the customer model.
3. Infer needs from behavior only when confidence is clear.
4. Let customers correct or declare preferences.
5. Connect needs to treatment, content, service, or product recommendations.

## Software Artifacts

- Needs taxonomy.
- Preference model.
- Intent tags.
- Needs-based segments.
- Needs-to-treatment mapping.

## Acceptance Checks

- Needs are actionable, not decorative labels.
- The system can distinguish value from need.
- Customers can update important preferences.
- Needs influence workflow or personalization decisions.
